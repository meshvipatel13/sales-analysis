# 📊 Sales & Customer Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance and customer behavior. The dashboard enables stakeholders, managers, and business executives to monitor key business metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The project consists of two dashboards:

* Sales Dashboard
* Customer Dashboard

Built using Tableau, the dashboard provides year-over-year comparisons, trend analysis, customer insights, and interactive filtering capabilities.

---

## 🎯 Business Requirements

### Sales Dashboard

The Sales Dashboard provides an overview of business performance by analyzing:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity Sold
* Year-over-Year Performance
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Product Subcategory Performance
* Weekly Sales & Profit Trends

### Customer Dashboard

The Customer Dashboard focuses on customer behavior and engagement by analyzing:

* Total Customers
* Sales per Customer
* Total Orders
* Customer Trends
* Customer Distribution by Orders
* Top 10 Customers by Profit
* Customer Purchase Patterns
